etytree : a visual etymology dictionary using trees




etytree can visualize the etymological tree of a word, i.e., the etymology - or the origin of a word and the historical development of its meaning - in the form of a tree, reconstructing its ancestral form, its cognate words, its derived words, etc. This first version uses directed graphs instead of trees because of the nature of the current data.

This version implement automatic extraction and parsing of data from the English Wiktionary.

This project is open source (source code) and uses d3 and Wiktionary.
Anyone can directly contribute or indirectly contribute by improving the etymological entries of words inthe English Wiktionary.
