etytree
A graphical and multilingual etymology dictionary based on Wiktionary

What is etymology?

Etymology is the origin of a word and the historical development of its meaning.

- The Oxford English Dictionary




Etytree is the first graphical and multilingual etymology dictionary.
With etytree you can visualize the steps that led to the formation of a word as we know it, and
discover words that share one or more ancestors with that word in the same or in a different language.


Use etytree to discover how English tear and Italian lacrima (which means tear) share a common ancestor.
